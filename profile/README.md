## Overview

DVDpedia Advanced is a movie and DVD collection management application from Bruji that catalogs physical disc collections and digital movie libraries with automatic metadata lookup, cover art retrieval, and a visual browsing interface that makes managing a film collection organized and enjoyable. It retrieves movie information from online databases by barcode scan, title search, or manual entry, building a comprehensive catalog without tedious data entry. The application serves movie collectors who want their collection organized, browsable, and trackable.

Automatic metadata lookup retrieves movie titles, directors, cast, genres, runtime, ratings, plot summaries, and cover art from online databases by scanning a disc barcode with the camera or searching by title. The lookup populates all relevant fields instantly, transforming collection cataloging from a data entry task into a scanning task. Multiple database sources ensure comprehensive coverage across international releases.

Collection browsing displays movies as visual cover art grids, sortable lists, or detailed views with filtering by genre, director, year, rating, format, and custom tags. Smart collections automatically group movies by criteria — unwatched films, favorites, specific genres, recent additions — without manual curation. The visual browsing experience makes choosing what to watch a pleasant exploration rather than a shelf scan.

Lending tracking records who has borrowed which items and when, providing accountability for loaned discs and helping track down items that have not been returned. The lending history maintains a complete record of each item's loan history. Overdue notifications remind borrowers (and lenders) when items should be returned.

For movie collectors who want their DVD, Blu-ray, and digital movie collections organized, browsable, and tracked with the minimum possible effort per entry, DVDpedia Advanced is the collection management tool that makes film cataloging fast and film browsing enjoyable.

## Performance

DVDpedia balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your system responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management and smart caching reduce overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series chips, delivering noticeably faster performance. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
